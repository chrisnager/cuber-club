Cube DJ

Use your Bluetooth-enabled smart cube as a MIDI input device.

Your moves

Hold cube with white on top and green facing you.

Sounds

  • R: Kick 1
  • R': Kick 2
  • L: Snare 1
  • L': Snare 2
  • U: Rim
  • U': Hi-hat 1
  • D: Hi-hat 2
  • D': Hi-hat 3
  • F: Cymbal
  • F': Bongo high
  • B: Bongo low
  • B': Conga low

Currently, Web Bluetooth support is limited to Chrome, Opera, and Edge.
